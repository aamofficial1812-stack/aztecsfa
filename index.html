About Us

MORE THAN ACADEMY,
WE ARE A FAMILY.

Welcome to Aztecs Mumbra Football Academy — the heart of grassroots football in Thane. Founded with a passion to ignite the spark of football in every child, Aztecs isn't just an academy — it's a movement. A movement that turns playground passion into disciplined performance, and weekend goals into lifelong ambition.

From narrow lanes to open fields, we're shaping the next generation of players with a bold vision: to create not just athletes, but leaders, dreamers, and team players. Whether you're a beginner learning to pass or a rising talent preparing for tournament glory, our certified coaches, structured training, and relentless energy make every session count.

Our Team

THE ARCHITECTS OF CHAMPIONS

World-class mentors shaping the next generation

Founder & Head Coach
MR. SHOEB VINDANI

Coach Shoeb, an AIFF 'D' License certified coach, boasts a strong playing career with clubs like Mumbai FC (U-19), Bank of Baroda (Elite Division Champions), and Dena Bank (Super Division Winners). He has represented Thane and Mumbai District teams, participated in the Santosh Trophy Maharashtra Camp, and won several national tournaments. As a Bajaj Allianz U-17 selector, he now mentors young players with experience, discipline, and passion.

AIFF 'D' License Mumbai FC Alumni U-17 Selector
Coach
MR. YAKUB ANSARI

Yakub Sir is a passionate football mentor who has represented Bombay Muslim and played in the Colaba Senior Division. He attended the U-16 India Camp and participated in the Sub-Junior Nationals. Honoured with a ₹5,000 award, his dedication and experience make him a strong pillar in youth football, inspiring the next generation of players.

Bombay Muslim FC U-16 India Camp Sub-Junior National
