About Us

More Than Academy,
We Are A Family.

Welcome to Aztecs Mumbra Football Academy — the heart of grassroots football in Thane. Founded in 2021 with a passion to ignite the spark of football in every child, Aztecs isn't just an academy — it's a movement. A movement that turns playground passion into disciplined performance, and weekend goals into lifelong ambition.

From narrow lanes to open fields, we shape the next generation with a bold vision: to create not just athletes, but leaders, dreamers, and team players. Our certified coaches, structured training, and relentless energy make every session count. At Aztecs, football is more than a game — it's a way of life.

Our Team

The Architects
Of Champions

World-class mentors, certified coaches, and lifelong builders of the beautiful game.

Founder & Head Coach
Mr. Shoeb Vindani

An AIFF 'D' License certified coach with a commanding playing career at Mumbai FC (U-19), Bank of Baroda (Elite Division Champions), and Dena Bank (Super Division Winners). He has represented Thane and Mumbai District teams, participated in the Santosh Trophy Maharashtra Camp, and won multiple national tournaments. As a Bajaj Allianz U-17 Selector, he now mentors young players by combining experience, discipline, and passion to shape future football stars.

AIFF 'D' License Mumbai FC U-19 Santosh Trophy U-17 Selector National Tournaments
Senior Coach
Mr. Yakub Ansari

A passionate football mentor who has represented Bombay Muslim and played in the Colaba Senior Division. He attended the U-16 India Camp and participated in the Sub-Junior Nationals. Honoured with a ₹5,000 award, his dedication and experience make him a strong pillar in youth football, inspiring the next generation of players with tireless commitment, expertise, and a burning love for the beautiful game.

Bombay Muslim FC India U-16 Camp Sub-Junior Nationals Award Recipient
