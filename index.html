About Us

MORE THAN ACADEMY,
WE ARE A FAMILY.

Welcome to Aztecs Mumbra Football Academy — the heart of grassroots football in Thane. Founded with a passion to ignite the spark of football in every child, Aztecs isn't just an academy — it's a movement. A movement that turns playground passion into disciplined performance, and weekend goals into lifelong ambition.

From narrow lanes to open fields, we're shaping the next generation of players with a bold vision: to create not just athletes, but leaders, dreamers, and team players. Whether you're a beginner learning to pass or a rising talent preparing for tournament glory, our certified coaches, structured training, and relentless energy make every session count. At Aztecs, football is more than a game — it's a way of life.

Our Programs

PROGRAMS FOR EVERY PLAYER

Grass Root Program

Ages 5 – 7 yrs

Nurturing the very first love for the beautiful game. Fun-first sessions focused on coordination, motor skills, and building a lifelong bond with football.

Junior Program

Ages 8 – 11 yrs

Technical foundations, ball mastery, and team dynamics. Players develop core skills in a structured yet playful training environment.

Youth Development

Ages 12 – 14 yrs

Tactical awareness meets physical conditioning. Advanced drills, positional training, and competitive match preparation for rising talents.

Elite Performance

Ages 14 – 16 yrs

High-intensity training that mirrors professional standards. Scouts, tournaments, and elite competition exposure for the most dedicated players.

Seniors Batch

Ages 16+ yrs

Professional-grade training, fitness regimes, and tournament preparation for senior players pushing toward competitive football careers.

Goal Keeper Program

All Age Groups

Specialist goalkeeper coaching — reflexes, distribution, positioning, and mental resilience. Develop elite shot-stoppers from any age group.

Our Team

THE ARCHITECTS
OF CHAMPIONS

Founder & Coach

Mr. Shoeb Vindani

An AIFF 'D' License certified coach with a strong playing career at Mumbai FC (U-19), Bank of Baroda (Elite Division Champions), and Dena Bank (Super Division Winners). He has represented Thane and Mumbai District teams, participated in the Santosh Trophy Maharashtra Camp, and won several national tournaments. As a Bajaj Allianz U-17 Selector, he now mentors young players by combining experience, discipline, and passion to shape future football stars.

Coach

Mr. Yakub Ansari

A passionate football mentor who has represented Bombay Muslim and played in the Colaba Senior Division. He attended the U-16 India Camp and participated in the Sub-Junior Nationals. Honoured with a ₹5,000 award, his dedication and experience make him a strong pillar in youth football, inspiring the next generation of players with tireless commitment and expertise.

Building champions on and off the pitch. Aztecs Football Academy — the heart of grassroots football in Thane, shaping the next generation of leaders.
