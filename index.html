About Us

MORE THAN ACADEMY,
WE ARE A FAMILY.

Welcome to Aztecs Mumbra Football Academy — the heart of grassroots football in Thane. Founded with a passion to ignite the spark of football in every child, Aztecs isn't just an academy — it's a movement. A movement that turns playground passion into disciplined performance, and weekend goals into lifelong ambition.

From narrow lanes to open fields, we're shaping the next generation of players with a bold vision: to create not just athletes, but leaders, dreamers, and team players. Certified coaches, structured training, and relentless energy make every session count.

Our Team

THE ARCHITECTS OF CHAMPIONS

World-class mentors shaping the next generation

Founder & Head Coach
MR. SHOEB VINDANI

An AIFF 'D' License certified coach with a strong playing career including Mumbai FC (U-19), Bank of Baroda (Elite Division Champions), and Dena Bank (Super Division Winners). He has represented Thane and Mumbai District, participated in the Santosh Trophy Maharashtra Camp, and served as a Bajaj Allianz U-17 selector.

AIFF 'D' License Mumbai FC Alumni U-17 Selector
Coach
MR. YAKUB ANSARI

A passionate football mentor who represented Bombay Muslim and played in the Colaba Senior Division. He attended the U-16 India Camp and participated in the Sub-Junior Nationals. Honoured with a ₹5,000 award, his dedication makes him a strong pillar in youth football.

Bombay Muslim FC U-16 India Camp Sub-Junior National
