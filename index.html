About Us

More Than Academy,
We Are A Family.

Welcome to Aztecs Mumbra Football Academy — the heart of grassroots football in Thane. Founded with a passion to ignite the spark of football in every child, Aztecs isn't just an academy — it's a movement that turns playground passion into disciplined performance, and weekend goals into lifelong ambition.

From narrow lanes to open fields, we shape the next generation with a bold vision: to create not just athletes, but leaders, dreamers, and team players. Our certified coaches, structured training, and relentless energy make every session count.

Our Programs

Programs For
Every Player

Six pathways to excellence, crafted for every stage of the beautiful game.

01

Grass Root Program

Ages 5 – 7 Years

Nurturing the very first love for the beautiful game. Fun-first sessions focused on coordination, motor skills, and a lifelong bond with football.

02

Junior Program

Ages 8 – 11 Years

Technical foundations, ball mastery, and team dynamics. Players develop core skills in a structured yet playful training environment.

03

Youth Development

Ages 12 – 14 Years

Tactical awareness meets physical conditioning. Advanced drills, positional training, and competitive match preparation.

04

Elite Performance

Ages 14 – 16 Years

High-intensity training mirroring professional standards. Scouts, tournaments, and elite exposure for the most dedicated players.

05

Seniors Batch

Ages 16+ Years

Professional-grade training, fitness regimes, and tournament preparation for senior players pushing toward competitive careers.

06

Goal Keeper Program

All Age Groups

Specialist goalkeeper coaching — reflexes, distribution, positioning, and mental resilience. Elite shot-stoppers at every age.

Our Team

The Architects
Of Champions

World-class mentors. Certified coaches. Lifelong builders of the game.

Founder & Head Coach

Mr. Shoeb Vindani

An AIFF 'D' License certified coach with a commanding playing career at Mumbai FC (U-19), Bank of Baroda (Elite Division Champions), and Dena Bank (Super Division Winners). He has represented Thane and Mumbai District teams, participated in the Santosh Trophy Maharashtra Camp, and won multiple national tournaments. As a Bajaj Allianz U-17 Selector, he now mentors young players by combining experience, discipline, and passion to shape future football stars.

Senior Coach

Mr. Yakub Ansari

A passionate football mentor who has represented Bombay Muslim and played in the Colaba Senior Division. He attended the U-16 India Camp and participated in the Sub-Junior Nationals. Honoured with a ₹5,000 award, his dedication and experience make him a strong pillar in youth football, inspiring the next generation with tireless commitment and expertise.
